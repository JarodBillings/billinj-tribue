Paypal

After the sale of his first company, Zip2 in 1999, Elon used the from the sale to create his a new company X.Com. One year later the new company was aquired by the software company Cofinity, which had a money-transfer function named "Paypal". In 2002, after years of growth, the service was aquired for $1.5B from Ebay. Since that time the company has exploded to a valuation of $105B.

Space Exploration Company

After stepping down as the CEO of Paypal in 2002, Elon focused his efforts on a more ambitous project. Musk had started the Mars Oasis project with the goal of creating a greenhouse on the planet Mars. After meeting with companies to purchase the rockets needed to get a spacecraft to Mars, Musk was frusted at the high cost of a project. Concluding that he could get the job done better, Musk formed SpaceX, with a new goal of habiting the red planet. In 2015 at the head of Musk, SpaceX was able to launch a rocket and succesfully land it pack onto the launch pad.

Tesla Motors

With the thought of space travel on his mind, Musk decided to also create a more down to earth project. The goal of this new project was the design and engineering of an all electric vehicle. Although many electric cars had been tried before, it was only until Musk's innovation did they become a reality. Following seven years of research and development, Tesla Motors released the Tesla Roadster. This was the first all electric-car to travel more than 200 miles per charge. Since that, Tesla has focused on designing a car to be fully self-driving. As of 2019, Telsa Motors has raised $44B in valuation to make this a reality.
